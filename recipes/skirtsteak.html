Skirt Steak

Description

This skirt steak recipe is quick and easy to put together and only needs to marinade for 30 minutes. The perfect solution when you lose track of time and need to put something on the table in a hurry!

It is best if you have a grill capable of reaching higher temperatures of 600+ degrees - perhaps with a sear option - so that the honey carmelizes on the outside. Most grill recipes I use rely on temperature, but I found it is best to just follow a general time of 2-3 minutes per side. You may need to practice a few times to get it right!

Ingredients

  • Skirt steak - 16 oz
  • Balsamic vinegar - 1 Tbsp
  • Olive oil - 1 1/2 Tbsp
  • Soy Sauce - 1 1/2 Tbsp
  • Honey - 1 1/2 Tbsp
  • Salt - 1/4 tsp
  • Pepper - 1/4 tsp
  • Garlic (coarsely chopped) - 1 clove

Steps

  1. In a mixing bowl, add balsamic vinegar, olive oil, soy sauce, honey.
  2. Crush a clove of garlic and coarsely chop. Add to bowl.
  3. Add salt and pepper.
  4. Trim any excess fat from skirt steak.
  5. Place skirt steak in a sealable container and pour contents of marinade onto steak. Close container and refrigerate if needed.
  6. Let steak marinade for at least 30 minutes. Remove from refrigerator 30 minutes prior to grilling.
  7. Preheat grill to highest setting.
  8. Sear and grill on one side for 2-3 minutes. Flip and sear/grill for 2-3 minutes. Remove from grill.
  9. Let sit for five minutes prior to cutting.
  10. Identify the grain and be careful to cut against the grain. You may need to cut in sections.

Teriyaki Chicken

Description

This recipe is a great, simple solution for a weeknight meal. The marinade goes together easy and there is minimal prepwork involved. Perfect for an everyday meal.

The marinade should sit for 2-3 hours, but sometimes we can't wait that long! One hour is ok, but we advise at least two hours - the longer the better! We usually serve this meal with rice and steamed broccoli.

Ingredients

  • (4) chicken breasts (skinless,boneless)
  • Honey - 2 Tbsp
  • Soy sauce - 1/2 cup
  • Rice wine vinegar - 2 Tbsp
  • Garlic - 3 cloves
  • Fresh ginger - 1 Tbsp
  • Brown sugar - 2 Tbsp
  • Sesame oil - 2 Tbsp

Steps

  1. Peel fresh ginger, mince.
  2. Crush garlic, mince.
  3. In a mixing bowl, add soy sauce, rice wine vinegar, and sesame oil.
  4. Stir in honey and brown sugar. Make sure there is no clumping together.
  5. Crush a clove of garlic and coarsely chop. Add to bowl.
  6. Add garlic and ginger. Mix together.
  7. Remove chicken from packaging and place in a sealable container. Add marinade on top of chicken and massage into chicken. Seal container and refrigerate.
  8. Let steak marinade for at least 2-3 hours. Remove from refrigerator 30 minutes prior to grilling.
  9. Preheat grill.
  10. Grill on one side for 2-3 minutes. Flip and grill for 2-3 minutes. Remove from direct heat and place on grill side with indirect heat. Grill until fully cooked - 165 degrees. Remove from grill.
  11. Enjoy!
